üî¨ Research Areas

Foundation Models & Advanced ML

Large Language Models: Co-developed from 1B to 100B parameter foundation models with novel reasoning architectures. Research on mathematical reasoning, rule-based computation, and reliability in LLM outputs.

Model Generalization: Revolutionary optimal transport-based domain adaptation methods (CVPR 2020, 200+ citations). Solved critical transfer learning challenges enabling models to generalize across domains.

Multimodal AI: Novel MetaRuleReasoner framework and multimodal logical reasoning chains across text, image, and structured data.

AI for Scientific Discovery

Materials Science: S2SNet - First AI achieving 92% accuracy in superconductor discovery. Published in IJCAI 2022 and NeurIPS 2022.

Chemistry & Biology: Comprehensive survey of scientific LLMs (ACM Computing Surveys, IF: 23.8). Retrosynthesis prediction using iterative string editing (Nature Communications).

Physics-Informed Neural Networks: E(2)-equivariant vision transformers leveraging group theory. Spatial coherence instructed swin transformer applied for universal holographic imaging.

Production ML Systems

Recommendation Systems: Novel time-series recommendation addressing dynamic missing data (MNAR) challenges. Published in IEEE TKDE (CCF-A, IF: 11.6).

Enterprise Applications: Full lifecycle user behavior modeling for e-commerce (KDD 2023, CCF-A). Scalable systems serving millions of users.
